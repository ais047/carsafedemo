World Safety Drive

The Latin “co” prefix means working together, e.g., co-operation, coed, coop. Intelligent Driving is a driving safety cooperative course. Humans do not have a stare at the road button. Road concentration and distraction avoidance require discipline for an intense concentration effort, and it helps to know this! The Copilot Driver License shares important road concentration, and driving safety training and insight that benefit drivers, passengers, and all on the road! On the road we all copilot.

Passenger Alert

Copilot Driver License training turns a potentially dangerous passenger seeking eye contact, into a helpful copilot, watching the road, handling communications and navigation, while possibly assisting with lane change or parking. When talking in person people naturally seek eye contact, and this is true even while driving. Talking hands-free on a mobile phone while driving, is safer than talking to a passenger seeking eye contact! For this reason, passengers and children should be warned not to distract the driver. Teach driving cooperation and turn a potentially dangerous passenger into a driving safety asset!

Join the Copilot Club free by simply reading the Copilot Driver License, watching the videos, and sending this page to family, friends, and other new copilots. Driving cooperation makes the roads safer. The safer driving you are, the safer others will be! Help protect the ones you love.
